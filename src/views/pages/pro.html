Your information shall be processed for specific, explicit, and legitimate purposes, including:

LegalHuB shall not sell your personal data. We may disclose your information in specific situations:

  • Payment Processors: To securely process payments for any subscription services.

Data Retention

LegalHuB shall retain your personal information only for as long as it is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Notice, which is typically as long as you maintain an account with us. Security Measures

We have implemented appropriate, industry-standard technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. This includes using encryption for data in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest, secure server configurations, access controls, and other safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you have certain rights regarding the personal information we hold about you:

  • Right of Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we collect from you.
  • Right to Correction: You may request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

The Services use cookies, web beacons, and other similar tracking technologies to function, maintain your session, and gather analytics data. These are small data files that help the site work and allow us to understand user behavior. For more detailed information, please reference our separate Cookie Policy.

Controls for Do-Not-Track Features

Most web browsers and some mobile operating systems include a Do-Not-Track ("DNT") feature. At this stage, no uniform technology standard for recognizing and implementing DNT signals has been finalized. As such, we do not currently respond to DNT browser signals.
